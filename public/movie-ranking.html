The Best Movies According to Troy

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Alien

In the depths of space, no one can hear you scream... When the crew of the spaceship Nostromo responds to a distress signal from a desolate planet, they uncover a horrifying secret: an alien life form that is as deadly as it is relentless. As the creature stalks them one by one, survival becomes their only mission. Starring Sigourney Weaver as the fearless Ripley, Alien is a heart-pounding sci-fi horror classic that will leave you breathless. The ultimate fight for survival begins now.

Jurassic Park

In a world where science pushes the boundaries of nature... A visionary billionaire creates a theme park like no other — Jurassic Park. On a remote island, dinosaurs roam the earth once more, brought to life by cutting-edge genetic engineering. But when an elite group of experts and a curious pair of kids are invited to preview the park, what begins as a dream quickly turns into a nightmare. The power goes out. The cages are unlocked. And now, the hunters... become the hunted. From director Steven Spielberg, Jurassic Park is a heart-pounding adventure where survival is no longer about man versus man — it's about man versus the might of prehistory. Hold onto your butts. The dinosaurs are back.

The Shining

In a world where isolation turns sinister… From the master of horror, Stephen King, comes The Shining, a chilling tale brought to life by the legendary Stanley Kubrick. Jack Torrance, a struggling writer and family man, takes a job as the winter caretaker of the eerie Overlook Hotel, nestled high in the Colorado Rockies. But as the snow traps them inside, the hotel’s dark secrets awaken—and so does Jack’s descent into madness. Witness the haunting visions of his psychic son, Danny, and the creeping terror that tears a family apart. Will they survive the sinister whispers of the Overlook, or will Jack succumb to its malevolent grip? Redrum is just the beginning... “Here’s Johnny!”
